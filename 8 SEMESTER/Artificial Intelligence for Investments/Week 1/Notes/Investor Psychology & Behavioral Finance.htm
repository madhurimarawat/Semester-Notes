Investor Psychology & Behavioral Finance 💰

Concepts 📜 Description 📖
Market Inefficiency ⚖️ Prices often deviate from fundamental values due to irrational behavior.
Risk Attitude 🎲 Investors are more averse to losses than they are attracted to gains.
Prospect Theory 📊 People weigh potential losses more heavily than equivalent gains.
Probability Errors 🎯 Investors misjudge probabilities, often placing too much emphasis on recent events.
Belief Updating 🔄 Investors are slow to adjust their beliefs when new information emerges.
Overconfidence 🚀 Many investors believe they are better-than-average at picking stocks.
Anomalies & Bubbles 🎈 Psychological biases contribute to market anomalies and speculative bubbles.
Limits to Arbitrage 💹 Constraints that prevent investors from correcting mispriced assets.
Arbitrage Risk ⚠️ While arbitrage aims for risk-free returns, real-world scenarios often involve risks.
